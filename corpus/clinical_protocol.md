# Clinic Q1 2025 Protocol Notes

## Population
Our practice serves an adult primary care population (ages 18-90) in a suburban US setting. The cohort skews older (median age approximately 55) with elevated cardiometabolic risk profiles compared to national averages.

## Screening Cadence
Routine screening for diabetes (HbA1c) and hypertension (blood pressure) is performed at every annual visit. Patients with prior abnormal readings are followed at three-month intervals.

## Known Population Bias
Our cohort has higher-than-average BMI due to a recent intake from a bariatric referral program. Approximately 5% of new intakes since January 2025 carry BMI >= 40 at presentation. These cases are clinically expected for our patient mix and are not data quality artifacts.

## Q1 2025 Operational Notes
A calibration issue with one of the two blood-pressure cuffs was reported in February 2025; readings between February 10 and February 15 may show a slight upward bias. The cuff was recalibrated on February 15. Any apparent BP spike confined to that window should be cross-checked against the equipment log before clinical interpretation.
